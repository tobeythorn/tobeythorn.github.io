What is Protowrite?

A plain text to HTML converter

Protowrite is a plain text to HTML converter. It allows you to write in plain text without any markup, and then convert your documents to HTML. The output is suitable for professional publication. That might sound impossible, but it's not, and Protowrite is here to prove it.

Protowrite was created to make writing for online publication better. It caters to humans, not machines.

A plain text syntax

Imagine how you would naturally write a document on a typewriter or in a plain text editor. That's basically the Protowrite syntax. Protowrite defines a plain text syntax based on newlines, tabs, and spaces. It has virtually no special characters. This makes source documents exceptionally easy to read, write, and edit.

And by the way, Protowrite's syntax allows writers to easily include images, audio, video, or any embedded content in their documents.
